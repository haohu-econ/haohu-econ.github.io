Welcome to Hao Hu's website

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Department of Economics, University College London.

My research interests lie in development economics and labor economics; more specifically, I want to understand the matching and sorting in developing countries' labor markets, and how they can affect the organization of production. I am also interested in political economy.
